How to Play

Controls

Use your mouse or touch to move your paddle. You can only move on your side of the rink.

Objective

Score 5 goals before the AI or before the timer runs out! Hit the puck with your paddle to send it toward the opponent's goal.

Rules

The game lasts for 3 minutes. If time runs out and you haven't scored 5 goals, the AI wins. Good luck!

About Harky Hockey

Harky Hockey is a fast-paced, arcade-style ice hockey game built with modern web technologies. Our goal is to provide a smooth, responsive, and fun gaming experience directly in your browser without any downloads required.

Whether you're looking for a quick break or a challenging match against our AI, Harky Hockey offers an engaging way to test your reflexes and strategy.
